Crunchy Nigerian Buns

Descriptions

Nigerian Buns is a staple snack in almost all parts of Nigeria. This crunchy tasty and rich delight has brought(and still bringing) joy to many.

Ingredients

  • 450G(3 1/2 cup) plain flour
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 70g (1/3 cup) sugar
  • 1/4 nutmeg or cinnamon
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 125 ml (1/2 cup) whole milk or evaporated milk

Steps

  • Chop 3/4 of the Okras(okro pods) you’ll be using and blend or grate the remaining ones. And set aside
  • Slice the green vegetables of choice. Set aside
  • Leave to rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
  • Blend the red bell peppers(tatashe) plus the scotch bonnet peppers(atarodo) and a little onion. Set aside
  • Clean the fresh prawns as you like it. For this seafood okro soup, I’ll be taking out the prawn heads, removing the outer shells, deveining it , but leaving the tail for estethic purpose. But don’t throw away the heads yet.
  • Slice the fresh fish, remove the guts, rinse fish and set aside
  • YOU CAN NOW COOK AND ENJOY!
