Crunchy Nigerian Buns

Description

Nigerian Buns is a staple snack in almost all parts of Nigeria. This crunchy tasty and rich delight has brought(and still bringing) joy to many.

Ingredients

  • 450G(3 1/2 cup) plain flour
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 70g (1/3 cup) sugar
  • 1/4 nutmeg or cinnamon
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 125 ml (1/2 cup) whole milk or evaporated milk

Steps

  1. Chop 3/4 of the Okras( okro pods) you’ll be using and blend or grate the remaining ones. And set aside
  2. Slice the green vegetables of choice. Set aside
  3. Leave to rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Blend the red bell peppers(tatashe ) plus the scotch bonnet peppers( atarodo) and a little onion. Set aside
  5. Clean the fresh prawns as you like it. For this seafood okro soup, I’ll be taking out the prawn heads, removing the outer shells, deveining it , but leaving the tail for estethic purpose. But don’t throw away the heads yet.
  6. Slice the fresh fish, remove the guts, rinse fish and set aside
  7. YOU CAN NOW COOK AND ENJOY!
