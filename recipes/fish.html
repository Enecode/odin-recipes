Nigerian Peppered Fish

Description


Nigerian grilled peppered fish also known as Nigerian spicy grilled fish is one of my go to recipes when I want something quick and easy , yet presentable to serve to my guests.

Ingredient

  • 1 Whole fish(croaker, tilapia, mahi-mahi or Sea Bass fish)
  • 60ml vegetable oil
  • 2 medium sized red bell peppers
  • 1 medium sized green bell pepper
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 3 large cloves of garlic
  • 1 thumb sized ginger

Steps

  • PPour 60ml vegetable oil plus the red bell peppers
  • Heat up about 3 tablespoonful vegetable oil and gently pour in the blended pepper mix
  • Cook and fry/grill your meat ( beef/chicken/turkey )
  • Add the ”All spices ”(if using) or use a mixed herbs of your choice
  • While the sauce is cooling , rinse the fish, remove the gut, scales and fins and dab dry with a paper towel or a clean napkin.
  • Using a sharpened knife, make some slashes on each sides of the fish
  • Massage the pepper sauce generously on the fish and into all the slashes and spaces on the fish.
  • Cover with foil or place into a zip-lock bag and leave to marinate in the fridge for one hour or longer
