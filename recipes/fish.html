Nigerian Peppered Fishs

Description

Nigerian grilled peppered fish also known as Nigerian spicy grilled fish is one of my go to recipes when I want something quick and easy , yet presentable to serve to my guests.

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole fish(croaker, tilapia, mahi-mahi or Sea Bass fish)
  • 60ml vegetable oil
  • 2 medium sized red bell peppers
  • 1 medium sized green bell pepper
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 3 large cloves of garlic
  • 1 thumb sized ginger

Steps

  1. PPour 60ml vegetable oil plus the red bell peppers
  2. Heat up about 3 tablespoonful vegetable oil and gently pour in the blended pepper mix
  3. Cook and fry/grill your meat ( beef/chicken/turkey )
  4. Add the ”All spices ”(if using) or use a mixed herbs of your choice
  5. While the sauce is cooling , rinse the fish, remove the gut, scales and fins and dab dry with a paper towel or a clean napkin.
  6. Using a sharpened knife, make some slashes on each sides of the fish
  7. Massage the pepper sauce generously on the fish and into all the slashes and spaces on the fish.
  8. Cover with foil or place into a zip-lock bag and leave to marinate in the fridge for one hour or longer
