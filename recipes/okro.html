Okra Soup Recipes

Description

Sea food okra soup is loaded with heart healthy nutrients and vitamins and taste really good.

Ingredients

  • 20 large Okra pods
  • 2 cups or 200gr green vegetable(spinach, ugwu, kale or shoko)
  • 2 large Red bell peppers(tatashe)
  • 2 Scotch bonnet peppers(atarodo)
  • Onions
  • 1 teaspoon Cameroun pepper(optional)
  • Fresh fish( I used mackerel(titus)
  • 1 cooking spoonful(half cup) Palm oil (palmoil)
  • half cup Crayfish ( grinded)
  • Seasoning powder or cubes to taste and Salt to taste

Steps

  • Add the baking powder, salt ,sugar and a little bit of nutmeg
  • Add my whisked egg, butter and milk and mix until combined without over mixing
  • Then I cover and leave to rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
  • Scoop the batter with my oiled or wet hand and drop it gently into the hot oil
  • Reduce the heat and let the buns fry gently until the inside is properly cooked.
  • Check the ”doneness” of the buns by pricking it with the skewer to see if it’s cooked inside.
  • SERVE AND ENJOY!
