Okra Soup

Description

Sea food okra soup is loaded with heart healthy nutrients and vitamins and taste really good.

Ingredients

  • 20 large Okra pods
  • 2 cups or 200gr green vegetable( spinach, ugwu, kale or shoko)
  • 2 large Red bell peppers(tatashe)
  • 2 Scotch bonnet peppers( atarodo)
  • Onions
  • 1 teaspoon Cameroun pepper(optional)
  • Fresh fish( I used mackerel(titus)
  • 1 cooking spoonful(half cup) Palm oil (palmoil)
  • half cup Crayfish ( grinded)
  • Seasoning powder or cubes to taste and Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Add the baking powder, salt ,sugar and a little bit of nutmeg
  2. Add my whisked egg, butter and milk and mix until combined without over mixing
  3. Then I cover and leave to rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Scoop the batter with my oiled or wet hand and drop it gently into the hot oil
  5. Reduce the heat and let the buns fry gently until the inside is properly cooked.
  6. Check the ”doneness” of the buns by pricking it with the skewer to see if it’s cooked inside.
  7. SERVE AND ENJOY!
