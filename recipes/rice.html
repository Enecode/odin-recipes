Jellof Rice Recipes

Description

Nigerian Jollof Rice is probably the BEST jollof rice recipe in the world: rich and flavorful with a slight smokey taste that gives it its unique name as the Party Jollof Rice.

Ingredients

  • 4 whole tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 scotch bonnets peppers
  • 100ml vegetable oil
  • About 600ml Meat or Chicken stock
  • small onion (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoonful ground crayfish(optional)
  • Â½ teaspoon each of Thyme and curry

Steps

  1. Parboil(precook) the Rice and set aside
  2. Cook and fry/grill your meat ( beef/chicken/turkey )
  3. Cook and fry/grill your meat ( beef/chicken/turkey )
  4. Prepare a little stew or sauce
  5. Now Scoop out about a quarter of the stew and set it aside for later use
  6. Add the meat or chicken stock to the pot and leave to boil on high heat for 5-10 minutes
  7. Next, add the parboiled rice and mix thoroughly; the liquid in the pot should be at the same level as the rice in the pot
  8. When the liquid in the rice is almost dried, add the ground crayfish (this is optional) and the remaining stew to the top
